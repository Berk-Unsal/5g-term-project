Objective:
Containerize Open5GS and UERANSIM using Helm or manifests and deploy them in Kubernetes.
Description:
Students will understand microservice-based 5G cores by running them in Kubernetes. They’ll verify UE registration within the cluster.
Tools & Technologies:
Open5GS, UERANSIM, Kubernetes (K3s/Minikube), Helm, Docker
Deliverables:
Running 5G Core in Kubernetes
Working UE registration
Diagram of pods and services
